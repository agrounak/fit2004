Requirements
============
essay within 10000 words

no words appear more than 50 times

program needs to find the number of words in the story including a count of each word

word count should not include:
am, is, are, was, were, has, have, had, been, will, shall, may, can, would, should, might, could
and articles:
a, an, the

punctuation marks used will be comma, period, question mark, exclamation mark, colon, semicolon, quotation

program will show a list of the words, (sorted in alphabetical order with their counts)

Can also find the k top most frequent words that appear in the essay
